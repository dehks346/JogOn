About
Jog On

Jog On was founded by two local families who each lost a loved one to an undiagnosed heart condition. Andria lost her father at age 35, and Denise and Robin lost their son Liam at age 23. Jog On was created in their memory.

Our goal is to raise awareness and funds for Cardiac Risk in the Young (C.R.Y.), a charity dedicated to preventing sudden young deaths through screening, research, and awareness.

Our Beginning

From Concept to Anticipated Event

It began when Denise and Robin met Andria and Ed at their local pub and discovered they had both suffered similar losses due to undiagnosed heart conditions. This shared experience sparked the idea for a fundraiser.

The first Jog On Run attracted over 120 participants and raised more than ¬£3,700 for C.R.Y. The energy was infectious, the impact was real, and the event was destined to grow.
